## Overview

**Eiger CARE Loyalty** is a core module within the Eiger digital ecosystem, specifically designed to serve and reward the brand's customer base. The primary objective of this application is to maintain and significantly enhance customer loyalty by providing tangible benefits. 

Customers can engage with the platform to earn reward points and e-vouchers, which can then be seamlessly redeemed for transactions across all Eiger physical branches and digital touchpoints. These rewards are dynamically distributed through regular transactions, special marketing campaigns, and exclusive events.
