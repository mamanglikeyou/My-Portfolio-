## Overview

The **Product Information Management (PIM)** application is a _web_ platform developed to streamline the management of highly detailed product articles. 

Before this system, the process of enriching product data and publishing articles to various destination channels was overly complex and suffered from significant workflow bottlenecks. This PIM application resolves these problems by serving as the central hub. It standardizes how detailed information is filled, managed, and structured, thereby optimizing the entire publication pipeline.
