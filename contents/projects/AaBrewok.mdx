## ☕ AaBrewok — Coffee Shop Website

---

Welcome to AaBrewok, an exclusive coffee shop offering an unforgettable coffee experience!
This website is designed to provide customers with easy access to explore menus, make seat reservations, and enjoy exciting promotions from all AaBrewok outlets.

## 🌐 Project Overview

---

AaBrewok Web is an innovative digital platform that connects coffee enthusiasts with the best experience from five AaBrewok coffee outlets.
Featuring a modern design, intuitive structure, and interactive features, users can easily browse menus, book seats, and stay updated with the latest news and promotions.

## 🚀 Key Features

---

🧾 1. Menu Exploration
Displays a variety of coffee and food selections available across all outlets.
Each item includes descriptions and prices to help customers choose conveniently.

📅 2. Online Seat Reservation
An advanced reservation feature that allows customers to book seats online quickly and efficiently.
Simply fill out the required details, and a confirmation will be sent directly to the user’s email.

🔐 3. User Registration & Login
Users can create personal accounts for a more personalized experience and easily track their reservation history.

🛠️ 4. Admin Panel
Designed for management to oversee all AaBrewok outlets.
Admins can manage reservation data, update menus, and monitor seat availability effortlessly.
