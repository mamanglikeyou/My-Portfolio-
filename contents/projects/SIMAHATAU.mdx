## 🤖 SIMAHA'TAU — Mobile-Based Chatbot

---

Welcome to SIMAHA’TAU
This intelligent mobile chatbot application is designed to help users manage information, answer questions, and provide solutions quickly and accurately.
With Natural Language Processing (NLP) technology and modern AI integration, SIMAHA’TAU acts as a virtual assistant capable of understanding conversational context just like a human.

## 📱 Overview

---

SIMAHA'TAU is a mobile-based chatbot application that utilizes artificial intelligence to understand user questions and provide relevant responses.
It functions not only as a Q&A engine but also as an interactive platform that adapts its answers based on user preferences and needs.

## 🧭 Software Scope

---

The development of this application includes several key components:

💬 Chatbot Development
The chatbot is built using the Gemini API, enabling it to understand and respond to various user queries contextually.

🎨 User Interface (UI/UX) Design
Designed with a simple, intuitive, and user-friendly interface to ensure smooth and comfortable interaction on mobile devices.

🔗 Third-Party Service Integration
SIMAHA’TAU can connect with external AI services such as Gemini (Google AI) or other AI models to enhance its capabilities and information coverage.

## 🌟 Key Features

---

🧠 1. Natural Language Processing (NLP)
The chatbot can understand everyday human language, recognize conversation intent, and deliver natural, contextual responses.

🔍 2. Information Retrieval
Capable of searching multiple data sources and presenting relevant, fast, and accurate answers to user queries.

👤 3. Personalization
SIMAHA’TAU learns from previous interactions to understand user preferences and provide increasingly personalized and relevant responses over time.

⚙️ 4. AI Integration
Can be connected with modern AI models such as Gemini, ChatGPT, or other NLP-based systems for broader and deeper results.
